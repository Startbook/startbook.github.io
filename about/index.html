About


Startbook provides products and services that support entrepreneurship programmes and empower their founders. We currently have three main areas of focus:


Startbook App


Whilst our vision is ambitious, our initial product offering is a SaaS digital directory for entrepreneurship programmes. Features and benefits include:

  • Founders and programme administrators can seamlessly edit and manage programme participant data.
  • Relevant data is automatically displayed in a presentable design to enable easy communication with stakeholders.
  • Premium features including a mentor directory and mentor requests.

Startbook Services


Providing services to entrepreneurship programmes including operational support, event management and programme design.

We're taking our learnings from this work to feed into our product roadmap to further support the organisations we work with.


Startbook Partners


We're supporting communities of diverse founders by providing them with exlcusive access to deals, discounts and offers thanks to our network of partners.
