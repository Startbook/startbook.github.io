x Impact Pitch Day

The CVC x Oxford Impact Pitch Day is an annual pitch competition funded by the CVC Foundation seeking early-stage entrepreneurs connected to the University of Oxford that are making a positive impact in the world.

Eligible startups* have the chance to pitch for and receive an investment from the Oxford Seed Fund of up to £40,000, as well as the opportunity to receive feedback and mentorship from the CVC judges.

*Eligibility Criteria:

  • The team must have progressed beyond the concept/idea stage and at least have a solution or prototype they are actively testing with users
  • Startups must be tackling challenges in one of the following areas:
    • Health & Wellbeing
    • Climate & Sustainability
    • Financial Inclusion
    • Education & Employability
    • Responsible AI
  • Although not an absolute requirement, it is preferable that the founder(s) travel to London in June 2025 to attend the live pitch event in person at CVC's offices.

Live Pitch

Following the assessment of written applications, 4-5 finalists will be selected to pitch in person in June 2025 at CVC’s offices at CVC Capital Partners, 111 Strand, London, WC2R 0AG. Each pitch will be followed by Q&A with the CVC judges.

Pitches will be scored based on the below judging criteria; once the pitches have completed the CVC judges will provide feedback to the Oxford Seed Fund Investment Committee, who will ultimately decide the winner(s) of the competition. The available £40,000 may be invested into one finalist or split between multiple finalists.





Judging Criteria:

Criterion Description Weighting
Quality of the business idea How good is the customer challenge/opportunity? 20%
Quality of solution Do they have product market fit? 15%
Commercial viability of the business Can they sell enough product/service at a high enough price to cover their costs? 25%
Potential for social or environmental impact How impactful could this venture be? 20%
Credibility of founder Do they have any previous experience in the target sector, or assembled a team that has? 20%
