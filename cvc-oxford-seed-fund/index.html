CVC Young Innovator Awards
with Oxford Seed Fund

The CVC Young Innovator Awards are a series of annual pitch competitions taking place in different territories around the world, powered by the CVC Foundation.

In the UK, CVC partners with Oxford Seed Fund to seek early-stage entrepreneurs connected to the University of Oxford that are making a positive impact in the world.

Eligible startups* have the chance to pitch for and win an equity-free grant from a prize fund of £40,000, as well as the opportunity to received feedback nd mentorship from the CVC judges.


*Eligibility Criteria:

  • One of the founders must be a current student/staff member or alumnus of the University of Oxford; they must be under 30 years or if over, the business should have a social purpose helping young people under the age of 30
  • The team must have progressed beyond the concept/idea stage and at least have a solution or prototype they are actively testing with users
  • The business must be incorporated or with intention to incorporate in the UK with a UK-based business bank account

Pitch Event:

Following the assessment of written applications, four finalists will be selected to pitch in person on 15th May 2025 at CVC’s offices at CVC Capital Partners, 111 Strand, London, WC2R 0AG. There will be up to 8 minutes to pitch, followed by up to 8 minutes of Q&A with the judges.

Pitches will be scored based on the below judging criteria; once the pitches have completed the CVC judges will provide feedback to the Oxford Seed Fund Committee, who will ultimately decide the winners.


Judging Criteria:

Criterion Description Weighting
Quality of the business idea How good is the customer challenge/opportunity? 20%
Quality of solution Do they have product market fit? 15%
Commercial viability of the business Can they sell enough product/service at a high enough price to cover their costs? 25%
Potential for social or environmental impact How impactful could this venture be? 20%
Credibility of founder Do they have any previous experience in the target sector, or assembled a team that has? 20%
