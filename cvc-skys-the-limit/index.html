with

The CVC Young Innovator Awards with Sky's the Limit is an annual pitch competition funded by the CVC Foundation supporting early-stage entrepreneurs that are part of the Sky's The Limit community.

Eligible startups* have the opportunity to receive feedback and mentorship from the CVC judges as well as an equity-free grant from a finalist prize pool of...

$30,000

*Eligibility Criteria:

  • The team must have progressed beyond the concept/idea stage and at least have a solution or prototype they are actively testing with users

Live Pitch

Following the assessment of written applications, 4-5 finalists will be selected to pitch virtually via Zoom in June 2025. Each pitch will be followed by Q&A with the CVC judges.

Pitches will be scored based on the below judging criteria; once the pitches have completed the CVC judges decide the winner(s) of the competition. The available $30,000 may be awarded to one finalist or split between multiple finalists.

Judging Criteria:

Criterion Description Weighting
Quality of the business idea Problem is real, solution meets target customer needs and the idea has market potential 20%
Business viability Business has a sustainable business model that demonstrates it has potential to grow 25%
Traction Business has demonstrated market traction to evidence its success 15%
Potential for impact Business has potential/ambitions to have a net positive societal, social and/or environmental impact 20%
Credibility of founder Founder/team has/have the necessary skills and commitment to deliver the business plan 20%

How to Apply:

Click the "Apply Now" button above to register your interest and begin your application on the competition portal hosted on Startbook. Once registered, you'll be able to generate a digital pitch profile for your business, which will be assessed by the competition judges. If you have questions regarding the competition please contact the Startbook Support team on support@startbook.co.uk.
