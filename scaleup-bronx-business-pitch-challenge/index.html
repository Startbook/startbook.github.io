Deadline to apply: 30 Oct 2024 @ 12:00pm noon ET

The ScaleUp Bronx Business Pitch Challenge is an entrepreneurial competition jointly led by Accompany Capital and OceanFirst Bank seeking commercially-viable for-profit businesses in Bronx, that are ready to scale.

Eligible businesses* have the chance to pitch for and win a top prize of...

$10,000

Finalists will also receive mentoring and support with their business from our partners.


*Eligibility Criteria:

  • Location:
    • If your business has a physical presence, your main office or location of operation must be in Bronx, NY
      • or
    • If you have an online business or app, at least one of the founders must live in Bronx, NY
  • Revenue: Your business is already generating revenue
  • NY Incorporation: The business must be incorporated in the State of New York
  • Pitch Coaching Participation: The founder(s) must be willing to take part in pitch coaching, should they be selected for the Pitch Final
  • Pitch Final Participation: The founder(s) must be available to pitch in person at the Pitch Final
