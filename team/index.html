Team

The first iteration of Startbook was founded by Maxx Turing in December 2019; for the first few months the business idea started life as a founder community before the key business model pivoted to a SaaS provider for entrepreneurial ecosystems. In the summer of 2020 Maxx pitched the business idea at the CVC Young Innovator Awards and won a £20,000 grant to launch the business and make it a reality. Following the awards, Maxx incorporated STARTBOOK LTD in July 2020 and contracted a software engineering consultancy to build out the initial product offering. Maxx went full-time on the business in October 2020 after the MVP was completed to focus on spending more time working closely with clients and iterating on the product. As a solo founder, Maxx has built the business up supported by a team of part-time contractors who have helped to build Startbook into what it is today.
